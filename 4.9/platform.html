Preferences

Proxy settings migrated from GSettings instead of GConf The Preferences > General > Network Connections preference page, now uses GSettings to detect proxy settings instead of GConf. GConf has been deprecated for several years and isn't even available on some systems anymore. GSettings is the latest and up-to-date method of fetching system settings from GNOME.
